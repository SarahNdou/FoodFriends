Easy lemon chicken stir fry

Ingredients

  • 4 chciken breasts, diced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 red pepper, cut into chunks
  • 140g sugar snap peas

    • Preparation

    Step 1: In a jug, mix together the honey, lemon stock and soy, then set aside. Toss the chicken with the cornflour so it's completely coated. Heat the oil in a non-stick pan, then fry the chicken until it is crisp around the edges and golden brown.
    Step 2: Add the carrots and red pepper, then fry. Pour the stock into the pan, bring to a simmer, then add the sugar snap peas and simmer eveerything together for 5 minutes until the chicken is cooked and the vegetables are tender. Serve with noodles or white rice.
